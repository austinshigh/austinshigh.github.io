I have been self-employed as a general contractor since 2017. My work as a general contractor informed my decision to pursue a degree in software engineering. There are many parallels between being a skilled construction worker, and a software engineer, which I am always happy to talk about at length. But in the name of being clear, I will keep it short, loose and bulleted.

  • Having all of the tools accessible to solve a problem, but being short some amount of understanding, excites me.
  • The extent to which carpentry and remodel skills can be mastered is truly fascinating. I am less content when I do not feel like a beginner.
  • I enjoy the process of taking some intimidating goal, and sectioning it into managable portions, which are enjoyable in their own sense to complete.
  • Much like any software program, a home's value is determined by how closely it meets the customers needs, I enjoy this challenge.
