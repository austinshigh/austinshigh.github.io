Construction:

I have been working in residential and commercial construction since I was in college. By developing my skills in the custom home/remodel markets, I was able to function on quality over quantity in my delivery, while also learning through experience.
I was able to shape an interest in carpentry into a variety of business management experiences.
In the name of clarity I will keep it short, loose and bulleted:

  • Having all of the tools accessible to solve a problem, but being short some amount of understanding, excites me.
  • The extent to which carpentry and remodel skills can be mastered is truly fascinating. I am most content when I feel like a beginner.
  • I enjoy the process of taking intimidating project goals, and sectioning them into managable portions which are enjoyable in their own sense to complete.
  • A custom home's value is determined by how closely it meets a client's needs, I enjoy the frustration and often irony of this challenge.
