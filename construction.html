Construction:

I began working for a general contractor while I was in college. I then went on to work on custom residential builds in Colorado, commercial projects in Philadelphia, and historic restorations in Philadelphia suburbs.
The similarities between construction and software development drew me back to computer science. However, while I enjoy the challenges of managing construction projects, I am more excited by the depth and constant evolution of computer science.
I feel properly challenged when I find myself with the correct tools to solve a problem, but lacking some amount of understanding.
I am happiest when I am a beginner. And as I mentioned, the constant evolution of computer science promises to keep me on my toes.
I enjoy the process of taking intimidating project goals, and sectioning them into managable portions which are enjoyable in their own sense to complete.
